Project Overview

The AI-Powered Predictive Analysis for Climate Change Impact on Ecosystems and Urban Development aims to analyze and predict climate change effects using artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Objectives

  • To predict climate change impacts on various locations using AI models.
  • To provide a dashboard for real-time data visualization.
  • To help decision-makers plan for climate change mitigation.
